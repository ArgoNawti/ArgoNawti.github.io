SERPENTINE: By Bridget Riley, 1999

As a pioneer of the "Op Art" movement, Bridget Riley created "Serpentine", as part of a exhibition collective called "Bridget Riley, paintings from the 1960's and 70's, which would be presented at the Serpentine Gallery in London. She implemented the alternating blocks/shapes of color to optically toy with viewers perception, a dynamic she was well known for. To visit Riley's work or to purchase, Continue Here
